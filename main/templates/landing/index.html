About FOFviz

Senior year project

FOFVIZ is a web-based solution for financial forecasting in simplicity. We collect data from various authorities in Turkey (including Turkish Central Bank) and provide you with analysis capabilities including visualization, data-filtering, and forecasts using machine learning to foresee the future outcomes and fluctuations in Turkish economy and allow wiser and easier decision-making.

  • Storing and retrieving data provided by Central Bank of Republic of Turkey.
  • Retrieve, filter and visualize data using beautiful and easy-to-understand plots.
  • Perform statistical (ratio and index) calculations.
  • Utilize Machine Learning models for financial forecasting.
  • Perform scenario, sensitivity and statistical stress testing for financial stability analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I get started?

    Just open one of our functionalities and let the wizard guide you through

  • Do I Need to Register?

    Not necessarily. Try out the essential features on the wizard dashboard as a guest. You can register for additional tools and service.

  • What FOFVIZ can offer for me?

    The wizard provides you a simplified view of descriptive analysis and opportunity to come up with conclusions about the macroeconomic transactions in the industry.

  • What are the real sources of data?

    Data is derived from official authorities in Turkey including TCMB (Turkish Central Bank) and BDDK.
